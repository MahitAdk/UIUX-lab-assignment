About Our Fest

Hi everyone! This is our college fest website. I made this website for our annual fest.

Event Name: TechFest 2025

Date: March 15-17, 2025

Description: We are having a 3 day technical festival with lots of competitions and fun activities. There will be programming contests, robot fights, web design, and much more. Everyone is invited!

Why you should come:

  • Win prizes
  • Meet new people
  • Learn new things
  • Have fun
  • Free food (maybe)

List of Events

Here are all the events we are having:

  1. Programming Contest
    This is for people who know how to code. You have to solve problems using computer programs.

  5. Project Display

  6. AI Competition
    Use artificial intelligence to solve real problems.
    Prize: Rs. 6000

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: Do I need to pay money to participate?
A: No! Everything is free. We just want you to have fun and learn.

Q: Can I participate in multiple events?
A: Yes, you can join as many events as you want. Just make sure the timings don't clash.

Q: What should I bring?
A: Bring your laptop, charger, and student ID card. For robot fighting, bring your robot.

Q: Will you provide food?
A: Yes, we will have food stalls and some free snacks during breaks.

Q: Can students from other colleges come?
A: Yes! All college students are welcome. Just bring your college ID.

Q: Where can I stay if I'm from another city?
A: We can help you find nearby hostels or budget hotels. Contact us after registration.
